DoodlePaste

What is this?

DoodlePaste is a way to share Doodle World sets with anyone.

How does it work?

  1. Paste your team (from The Teambuilder), or type it out (using the correct format).
  2. Add your name, a title, or notes (all optional).
  3. Share

Having problems?

Check out the format to make sure you're doing everything correctly
